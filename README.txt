Project: Columbina Hyposelenia | Preparation Tracker by 7utauta
Version: 1.1 

[ DESCRIPTION ]
This is a web-based tracking tool designed to help players prepare for the unreleased Genshin Impact character, Columbina. It features a material checklist, a savings calculator for Primogems/Fates, and an automated Summary Card generator that creates shareable images for your personal use.

[ HOW TO USE ]
1. Check off items as you collect them.
2. Enter your current number Primogem and Intertwined Fate counts at the top.
3. Click "Generate Summary Card" to create and download a PNG snapshot of your progress.
4. OPTIONAL, share it to your friends or in social media.

Note: Your progress is saved automatically to your browser's local storage.
